About Us

Driven by the Details

We love the 'numbers'. The details have been the backbone of our businesses and the cornerstone of every decision we've made.

The spark for PurchaseFlow came when we noticed a recurring blind spot in business spending across all company sizes, from startups to global enterprises. Too often, expenses slipped through without proper oversight, leading to inefficiencies and missed opportunities.

In our ongoing commitment to aid businesses in rectifying such inefficiencies, a startling statistic came to light: nearly one in three invoices contained discrepancies. Furthermore, requests for credit notes would frequently get lost amidst the myriad channels of communication, a problem that may well resonate with many businesses, perhaps even yours.

With these insights, PurchaseFlow was born. By putting an emphasis on proactive purchasing oversight, we're enabling businesses to sidestep financial pitfalls and capitalise on every penny.

PurchaseFlow is a cloud-based purchase order management system that helps businesses to streamline their purchasing processes. It is a simple, intuitive, and powerful solution that enables businesses to gain control and visibility over their spending.

Just a few of the problems PurchaseFlow solves are:

  • It helps you to track your spend against your budget so you dont overspend.
  • It sets any spend restrictions for your teams to work within, protecting your money and your business.
  • It helps you gain control over unauthorised spending.
  • It highlights and resolves out of contract price increases.
  • It helps you stay within budget and avoid overspending.
